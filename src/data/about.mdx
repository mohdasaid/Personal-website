Write your biography here. Tell the world about yourself. Link to your favorite [subreddit](https://www.reddit.com/). You can put a picture in, too. The code is already in, just name your picture `prof_pic.jpg` and put it in the `img/` folder.

Put your address / P.O. box / other info right below your picture. You can also disable any of these elements by editing the `author` property of `src/config/site.ts`. Edit `src/data/papers.bib` and the site will render your [publications page](/publications/) automatically.

Link to your social media connections, too. This template is set up to use [Font Awesome icons](https://fontawesome.com/) and [Academicons](https://jpswalsh.github.io/academicons/), like the ones below. Add your Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, Google Scholar, or just disable all of them.
