title: Unified Field Theory
description: An attempt to develop a single theoretical framework unifying gravitation and electromagnetism.
importance: 1

img: /assets/img/11.jpg
---

A long-standing research goal: finding a single field theory that encompasses both General Relativity (describing gravitation) and Maxwell's electromagnetism. This project explores geometric approaches to unification via higher-dimensional Riemannian manifolds.
