Started with high expectations but found the philosophical claims (M-theory as the "final theory")
were presented without sufficient justification, and the treatment of quantum gravity glossed over
too many open questions. Abandoned at Chapter 5.

The companion volume _A Brief History of Time_ remains essential reading.
